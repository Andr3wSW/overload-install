How to Use

Start Spy

Click the Start Spy button to record everything i-Ready does. This can assist us in further developing the menu to provide a better experience for you

Copy Spy Data

Whille Spy is active, click the Copy Spy Data button to copy a uniquely formatted string of code. Submit this to us by clicking the Submit Spy Data button on this page, and pasting your unique string into the form.

Skip Lesson

Click the Skip Lesson button to instantly skip a lesson on the My Path page. This will not result in a gain in minutes.

Auto 100%

Click the Auto 100% button to ensure you get 100% on a lesson. No matter how many questions you get wrong, after finishing a lesson you will get 100%

Farm Minutes

Click the Farm Minutes button to AFK without getting interupted. This essentialy prevents the Are You Still There message from occuring.

Diagnostic Hack

Click the Enable Diag Hack button to auto correct questions on the diagnostic. Enable this hack if you are unsure of a question.
